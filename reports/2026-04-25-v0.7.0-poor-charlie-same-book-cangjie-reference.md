## Conclusion

For v0.7.0, the same-book cangjie reference comparison is acceptable as a pragmatic downgrade path. It shows KiU's current Poor Charlie generated artifacts are not below the cangjie reference usage line and have a small usage-score lead under the internal benchmark. It does not re-evaluate or overwrite the v0.6.8 project-level cangjie absorption baseline.

The conclusion must remain narrow:

- Allowed claim: KiU v0.7 is competitive with the cangjie Poor Charlie reference on same-book usage scenarios.
- Not allowed: KiU has passed strict same-source-data A/B testing.
- Not allowed: KiU has fully absorbed cangjie methodology.
- Not allowed: KiU has external blind preference closure.
