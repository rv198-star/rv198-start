# Bias Self Audit Source Note
Skill ID: bias-self-audit
Primary Claim: Important decisions deserve an explicit bias checklist before commitment.
Evidence:
- Commitment, social proof, and incentive-caused bias should be named, not implied.
- The skill must be able to say when a low-stakes case does not justify a full audit.
- The output is a structured audit log with red flags and mitigating actions.
Source Provenance: Consolidated from the local KiU Notion export and reference traces in this bundle.
Original Notes: US Air regret and the surface-familiarity adversarial case are the strongest bundle anchors.
