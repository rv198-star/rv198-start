# Margin of Safety Sizing Source Note
Skill ID: margin-of-safety-sizing
Primary Claim: Position size should shrink as estimate quality falls or ruin risk rises.
Evidence:
- Margin of safety is not only about buy price; it also governs exposure size.
- Concentration is permitted only when downside, liquidity, and error bars are explicitly bounded.
- The skill should refuse when the user has not supplied uncertainty inputs.
