## Source Pollution Detection Method

`source_pollution_errors` is computed by scanning source-faithful `bundle/skills/*/SKILL.md` files for copied world-alignment summaries and world-alignment markers such as `world_context`, `No-Web Disclosure`, `web_check_performed`, and `world_context_isolated`. A passing score means the isolated `world_alignment/` layer did not leak into source-faithful skills.
