## Boundary Review

- External reference projects remain benchmarks only; no external final skill pack is ingested into the default pipeline.
- `workflow_script_candidate` artifacts are still excluded from `bundle/skills/`.
- `workflow-gateway` is a router only. It does not inline workflow steps or pretend deterministic procedures are thick skills.
- Graph navigation is used for handoff context, not as independent evidence.

## Residual Caveats

- Same-source usage delta over the local cangjie reference remains modest at `+1.3`; v0.6.1 should be described as distillation closure, not large usage overperformance.
- The Effective Requirements output is intentionally one gateway skill plus workflow candidates. That is acceptable for installability and boundary honesty, but it is not a claim that the book produced a deep agentic skill set.
- Further final-usage gains likely require downstream world-alignment or stronger semantic extraction, not just more substrate wiring.
